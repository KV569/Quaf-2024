MALABART
27th Batch of Darul Hidaya Da'wa College, class union (QUAF) Qualitative Union for Academic Focus, organized a class Artsfest under the name "MALABART." Inspired by history of Malabar Muslims.10/11/2024 The logo was launched by Ustad Abdul Kareem Thangal, Three teams and names were announced by class teacher Munavvar Hudawi. Coordinators: Ustad Nasik, Ustad Ramees, Ustad Mazin Sha.
Behind The Teams Name These Names (ARAKKAL, MARAKKAR, MAKHDOOM) were more important in the history of Malabar, especially, when the time of Portuguese and British colonialization. These dynasties were attacked against them to get freedom to India.
